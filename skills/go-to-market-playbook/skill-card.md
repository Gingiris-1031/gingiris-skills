## Description: <br>
A reusable Go-to-Market strategy template for B2B and B2C launches covering positioning, messaging, ICP definition, channel selection, and competitive analysis frameworks. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gingiris](https://clawhub.ai/user/gingiris) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Product, marketing, and growth teams use this skill to structure launch planning for B2B or B2C products. It helps an agent produce GTM planning guidance, worksheets, channel-market fit analysis, competitive battle-card structure, and messaging frameworks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The security evidence notes that only clean telemetry was available and recommends caution before granting broader authority. <br>
Mitigation: Install only when the publisher and purpose are recognized, and re-review the actual skill files before granting credentials, broad local file access, background execution, or mutation authority. <br>

Mitigation: Validate positioning, ICP scoring, messaging, and channel choices with current customer evidence before acting on the generated playbook. <br>
