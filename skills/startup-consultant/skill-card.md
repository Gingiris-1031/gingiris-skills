## Description: <br>
Startup growth consulting agent specializing in Product Hunt launches, open-source marketing, and go-to-market audits with strategic reviews and actionable recommendations. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External founders, startup operators, and growth leads use this skill to review Product Hunt launch plans, open-source growth assets, go-to-market positioning, and prioritized two-week growth sprints. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may generate marketing outreach, launch plans, or public posting recommendations that could be inaccurate or unsuitable for a specific audience. <br>
Mitigation: Review generated messaging and launch guidance before sending, publishing, or using it in a campaign. <br>
