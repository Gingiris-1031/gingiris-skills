## Description: <br>
A markdown launch guide that gives first-time Product Hunt makers a preparation timeline, launch-day schedule, and post-launch follow-up checklist. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External makers, founders, and launch teams use this skill to plan a Product Hunt launch, coordinate launch-day engagement, and convert post-launch traffic into retained users. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Launch outreach guidance could be applied as spam or in ways that violate Product Hunt, email, or community rules. <br>
Mitigation: Use personalized outreach, avoid unsolicited bulk messages, and review all emails, DMs, comments, and support requests against the relevant platform rules before sending. <br>
Risk: Marketing guidance may become outdated or may not fit every product, audience, or launch category. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the SOP as a planning checklist, verify timing and launch assets against current Product Hunt requirements, and adapt the plan to the product and audience before execution. <br>
