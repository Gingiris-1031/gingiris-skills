## Description: <br>
Startup Launch Day Playbook — Hour-by-hour execution checklist for launch day, covering timezone timing, channel sequencing, real-time crisis management, and post-launch momentum. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Founders and startup teams use this skill to coordinate launch-day execution from T-12 hours through the first 72 hours after launch. It provides checklists for final readiness, multi-channel activation, monitoring, amplification, and crisis response. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Launch guidance can trigger public posts, outreach, and payment-related checks that may affect a real business if followed mechanically. <br>
Mitigation: Review each checklist item with the launch team before execution and assign accountable owners for public communications, email outreach, and payment validation. <br>
