## Description: <br>
A step-by-step playbook for early-stage startups going from pre-seed to their first 1000 users, covering MVP validation, initial traction channels, and founder-led growth tactics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External founders and startup operators use this skill to plan early customer acquisition from MVP validation through the first 1000 users. It provides channel tactics, founder-led sales templates, traction metrics, and bootstrapping guidance in Markdown reference files. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>
