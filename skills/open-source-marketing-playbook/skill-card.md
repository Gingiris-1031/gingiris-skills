## Description: <br>
Marketing playbook for open-source projects led by non-technical founders, covering README optimization, community building, contributor attraction, and translating OSS traction into revenue. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gingiris](https://clawhub.ai/user/gingiris) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External founders and open-source maintainers use this skill to plan community launches, improve README conversion, attract contributors, and connect project traction to sponsorship or paid offerings. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Public launch posts, Reddit or Hacker News submissions, outreach messages, and requests for stars can be misleading or poorly timed if reused without review. <br>
Mitigation: Review and adapt all public-facing copy, channel choices, timing, and star requests to the actual project audience before publishing. <br>
