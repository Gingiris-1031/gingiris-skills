## Description: <br>
Beginner-friendly App Store Optimization guidance covering keyword research, screenshot design, rating management, localization, and A/B testing for iOS and Android apps. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gingiris](https://clawhub.ai/user/gingiris) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External developers, indie app publishers, and small mobile teams use this skill to get practical ASO guidance for improving app-store discoverability and conversion. It helps agents produce keyword research, screenshot optimization, rating strategy, localization, and A/B testing recommendations. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill includes external ClawHub and X links that lead outside the reviewed artifact. <br>
Mitigation: Review external links before following them and rely on the downloaded artifact as the reviewed source of skill behavior. <br>
Risk: ASO guidance can become stale or may not fit every app category, market, or store policy. <br>
Mitigation: Validate recommendations against current store data and run controlled A/B tests before applying changes broadly. <br>
