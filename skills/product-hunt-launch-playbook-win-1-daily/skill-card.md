## Description: <br>
Provides Product Hunt launch-day guidance covering launch timing, ranking factors, community mobilization, maker comments, and post-launch momentum. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External startup teams, founders, launch operators, and accelerator advisors use this skill to prepare and execute Product Hunt launch campaigns. It provides tactical planning guidance rather than code or automated platform actions. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill contains Product Hunt success claims and paid coaching or product links that may be marketing-oriented. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the claims as promotional context and independently evaluate whether the guidance fits the product, audience, and launch goals. <br>
Risk: Outreach or community engagement tactics could conflict with Product Hunt rules if applied too aggressively. <br>
Mitigation: Keep launch outreach compliant with Product Hunt rules and avoid spam-like coordination or misleading engagement. <br>
Risk: The skill references a separate install command for another skill. <br>
Mitigation: Review and scan any related skill before running separate installation commands. <br>
