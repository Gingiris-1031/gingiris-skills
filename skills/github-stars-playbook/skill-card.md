## Description: <br>
A 14-day sprint to rapidly grow GitHub stars for your repository, with tactical day-by-day guidance for Show HN, Reddit, Twitter/X threads, and GitHub Trending mechanics. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[gingiris](https://clawhub.ai/user/gingiris) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External developers and open-source maintainers use this skill to plan a two-week launch and promotion sprint for increasing GitHub repository visibility, community engagement, and star growth. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Marketing advice may conflict with platform-specific community rules or be perceived as spam if applied mechanically. <br>
Mitigation: Check each platform's current rules before posting and adapt outreach to genuine, useful technical content. <br>
Risk: Growth goals around stars, upvotes, and trending may encourage artificial amplification or direct vote solicitation. <br>
Mitigation: Avoid brigading, sockpuppets, spammy reposting, and direct requests for stars or upvotes; prioritize authentic community engagement. <br>
